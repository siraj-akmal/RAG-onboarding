Transactions – Equity Securities As a general rule, we execute client securities trades in the open market using broker-dealers acting on an agency basis. However, in certain circumstances, we may determine that it is prudent to sell certain illiquid holdings on a “block” trade basis where the broker is acting as principal. In such cases, we document our investment rationale for the execution, pricing and counterparty selection for such trade.
