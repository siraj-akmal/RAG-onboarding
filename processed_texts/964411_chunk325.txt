We also trade on non-exchange crossing networks such as alternative trading systems and multilateral trading facilities. In certain limited cases, we sell or buy securities to or from a broker-dealer off-exchange or where the dealer is acting on a principal basis. Over-the-Counter (OTC) Foreign Exchange. Foreign exchange trades are executed on a principal basis with approved counterparties. There is no explicit commission charged on foreign exchange trades as dealers are compensated by earning bid/ask spreads.
