Depending on the client portfolio, we engage in currency transactions on a spot (i.e., cash) basis at the rate prevailing in the currency exchange market and by entering into forward foreign exchange contracts. Certain portfolios allow for enhanced currency management (referred to as an “active strategy”) where the forward foreign exchange contracts are generally used to invest in currencies in the portfolio’s benchmark and opportunistically in currencies outside the portfolio’s benchmark to manage the currency exposure of the portfolio relative to the benchmark
