The SEC has adopted rules regarding the reporting of all short sale positions over a certain threshold to the SEC (and monthly public disclosure by the SEC of aggregated short sale data for issuers). In addition, many non-U.S. jurisdictions have rules requiring public disclosure of short positions, and additional rules could be adopted in other jurisdictions in the future.
