2 – Educational Background and Business Experience Mr. Berger (born 1989) currently serves as Arrowstreet’s Head of Portfolio Management and is a member of the firm’s Investment Committee. He has been with the firm since June 2013. Prior to Arrowstreet, Mr. Berger served as a derivatives trader at ABR Management LLC from January 2011 through May 2013 and at Toro Trading LLC from January 2010 through December 2010. Mr. Berger holds an MBA in finance from the Fordham Gabelli School of Business and a B.S. in business administration, finance and international business from Fordham University.
