6 – Supervision Arrowstreet’s investment process utilizes quantitative methods that focus on identifying and incorporating investment signals into its proprietary return, risk and transaction cost models. Portfolios are constructed through the use of a mean variance optimizer utilizing proprietary expected return, risk and transaction cost forecasts. Trading programs are generally implemented into client portfolios through scheduled rebalance sessions. Arrowstreet utilizes a team approach to the implementation of its investment process and the management of client portfolios. The firm’s Chief Investment Officer, Derek A. Vance, is responsible for the investment advisory activities of the firm and its team of investment professionals.
