Certain ESG voting matters are taken into account in our service provider’s standard benchmark proxy voting policies. In addition, upon the request of a client, we can implement (through the third-party service provider) enhanced ESG specific voting procedures with respect to the securities held in such client’s portfolio. For such clients, we contract with the service provider to cast votes based on a mutually agreed service provider specialized proxy voting policy.
