Foreign exchange trades are executed on a principal basis with approved counterparties. There is no explicit commission charged on foreign exchange trades as dealers are compensated by earning bid/ask spreads. We employ proprietary execution algorithms to execute against quotes provided by foreign exchange counterparties. We evaluate and execute each foreign exchange trade per our best execution policy, with the objective of minimizing the overall expected implementation cost. Exchange Traded Futures. Exchange traded equity index futures are executed via broker-dealer trading desks. Execution only commission rates are negotiated by contract and are generally consistent across broker-dealers. Commissions are calculated per lot
