It may be impossible to liquidate an existing position, to assess or value a position or to assess the exposure to risk. Risks Associated with Depositary Receipts. Depending on the client portfolio, we will purchase sponsored or unsponsored American Depositary Receipts, European Depositary Receipts and Global Depositary Receipts (collectively Depositary Receipts) typically issued by a bank or trust company which evidence ownership of underlying securities issued by a corporation. Generally, Depositary Receipts in registered form are designed for use in the U.S.
