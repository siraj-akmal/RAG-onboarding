Pursuant to guidance from the SEC and U.S. Department of Labor (DOL), we do not use commissions from client portfolio transactions to compensate brokers for absorbing a trade error or use one client portfolio to absorb an error in a different client portfolio. Additionally, consistent with the safe harbor under Section 28(e) of the Exchange Act, we will not compensate for a loss by providing future commissions or soft-dollars to a broker-dealer.
