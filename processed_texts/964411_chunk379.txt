As it relates to Arrowstreet Sponsored Funds, we do not accept voting directions or guidelines on an investor-by-investor basis. The authority to vote securities is typically set forth in the client’s investment management agreement or other written client communication. If such authority is vested in our firm, we engage an independent third-party proxy voting service to provide proxy voting services to the client portfolio.
