DUO Mobile What is DUO Mobile DUO Mobile is an extra secure form of two factor authentication. The app is downloaded to a phone and a unique activation code is sent to that phone with that phone number. So in the unlikely event that someone else gains access to your telephone number they still can't log into any services without the physical phone that DUO was installed on.
