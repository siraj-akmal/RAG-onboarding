Positions in underlying securities, coupled with an unanticipated increase in the value of the relevant currency, could expose a portfolio, even if fully or partially hedged relative to its benchmark, to unlimited losses. In portfolios where a prime broker is utilized, we may be required to direct a prime broker’s or a prime broker’s affiliate’s foreign currency trading desk to effect foreign exchange transactions to facilitate the settlement of equity trades or the repatriation of equity trade proceeds or cash balances in certain foreign currencies.
