Exchange traded equity index futures are executed via broker-dealer trading desks. Execution only commission rates are negotiated by contract and are generally consistent across broker-dealers. Commissions are calculated per lot in local currency units of the equity index futures contract. We monitor market practices and standards and re-negotiate commission rates as appropriate.
