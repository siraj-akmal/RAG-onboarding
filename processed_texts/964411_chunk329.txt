We believe that order aggregation may, where appropriate, allow us to execute trades in a more equitable manner and may reduce overall commission charges to client portfolios. There may, however, be instances in which order aggregation results in a less favorable transaction than a particular client portfolio would have obtained by trading separately. Similarly, when orders are not aggregated, there may be circumstances when purchases or sales of securities or contracts for one or more client portfolios will have an adverse effect on other client portfolios.
